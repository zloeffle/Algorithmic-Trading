Overview

Given a stock and date range, this page generates technical indicators for each date to help you make more calculated trading decisions. Technical indicators include simple moving average cross, relative strength index, and bollinger bands.

PRICE: Stock's closing price on the respective date
SHORT-MA: Short term simple moving average
LONG-MA: Long term simple moving average
BB-UPPER: Price range 2 standard deviations above the stocks 20 day moving average
BB-LOWER: Price range 2 standard deviations below the stocks 20 day moving average
BB-WIDTH: Width between the upper and lower bands
RSI: Relative strength index

Simply enter the ticker symbol for the stock of your choosing along with your desired start/end dates in YYYY-M-D format.
