Overview

Given a stock and date range, this page generates technical indicators for each date to help you make more calculated trading decisions. Technical indicators include simple moving average cross, relative strength index, bollinger bands, and overall trend direction.

PRICE: Stock's closing price on the respective date
SHORT-MA: 5-day simple moving average
LONG-MA: 15-day simple moving average
BB-SIGNAL: Signal generated by the bollinger bands indicator, 1 represents a BUY signal and -1 represents a SELL signal
RSI: Relative strength index
TREND: Overall trend direction on each day with respect to the starting date

Simply enter the ticker symbol for the stock of your choosing along with your desired start/end dates in YYYY-M-D format.
