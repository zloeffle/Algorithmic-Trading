Instructions

On this page, you can enter the ticker symbol for a stock, along with your desired date range, and see historical price data as well as buy/sell signals that are produced by two technical indicators, Relative Strength Index (RSI) and Bollinger Bands (BB). The purpose of this page is to help you gain a better understanding of historical price fluctuations for a stock, identify patterns, and see how these two technical indicators can be used to make predictions about what direction a stock may go in the future.

Simply enter the ticker symbol for the stock of your choosing along with your desired start/end dates in YYYY-M-D format. 1 represents a BUY signal, 0 represents HOLD, and -1 represents a SELL signal. Details about how these indicators work can be found by visiting this page: Learning
